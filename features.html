Key Features

MenuMatic is a QR-based food ordering system that provides a digital menu, real-time order tracking, and estimated wait times. Customers can order directly from their tables, receive digital receipts for flexible payments, and stay updated on order status. An admin dashboard simplifies order management and sales tracking.

QR-Based Ordering

Customers can conveniently place orders by scanning a QR code placed on their table, which directs them to the restaurant’s digital menu. This eliminates the need for waitstaff to take orders, speeds up the ordering process, and provides a contactless experience.

Order Status Updates

Customers are kept informed about their order's progress with real-time status updates—such as "Preparing," "Ready for Pickup," or "Served." This transparency minimizes confusion and helps customers stay aware of their order’s progress.

Real-Time Order Transmission

Once an order is confirmed, it is immediately sent to the kitchen dashboard, ensuring that the kitchen staff can begin preparation without delay. This direct connection minimizes errors associated with manual order-taking, improving service accuracy.

User Access Control

Allows restaurant managers to define roles and set specific permissions for staff members, ensuring secure access control over menu management, order processing, and dashboard functionalities, maintaining a well-organized and secure system.

Interactive Digital Menu

The platform offers a visually engaging menu where customers can view dish images, descriptions, prices, and available customizations. This includes options like adding extra toppings, adjusting portion sizes, and choosing sides, giving customers full control over their orders.

Estimated Waiting Time

After customers place an order, the system calculates and displays an estimated preparation time. This helps customers manage their expectations by knowing how long it will take for their food to be ready, reducing anxiety and improving their dining experience.

Customizable Menu Options

Upon completing their order, customers receive a digital receipt, which can be presented at the cashier for payment or handed to the waiter. This flexible option allows customers to choose their preferred payment method, streamlining the checkout process.

Admin Dashboard

Restaurant managers have access to an admin dashboard, where they can manage digital menus, view active and completed orders, generate sales reports, assign roles and permissions to staff, and customize system settings—all in one centralized platform.

User-Friendly Interface

The system is designed with a clean, responsive, and intuitive interface, ensuring that both customers and restaurant staff can easily navigate and use it. This user-centered design minimizes the learning curve and provides a smooth, efficient experience.
