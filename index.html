How It Works

Getting Started with MenuMatic

Initiating the Order via QR Code Scan

The process begins when a customer is seated at a table or approaches a counter where a MenuMatic QR code is prominently displayed. Instead of waiting for a physical menu or a staff member, the customer simply uses their smartphone camera to scan this QR code.

Accessing the Digital Menu

Upon scanning, the customer is instantly directed to MenuMatic's dedicated website for that specific restaurant. A key advantage here is that no app download is required, making it immediately accessible to virtually any smartphone user. On this responsive website, the customer finds the full digital menu, which can be interactive and visually appealing, much like Browse an online store.

Browse and Placing the Order

Customers can leisurely browse the menu at their own pace, viewing categories, item details, and potentially customization options (if the restaurant allows, like adding toppings or specifying preferences). They select the items they want and add them to a digital cart within the website interface. Once satisfied with their selection, they proceed to confirm and place their order through the system.

Providing Transparency and Updates

To enhance the customer experience, MenuMatic provides transparency after the order is placed. The system can display an estimated wait time for the order's preparation. Furthermore, customers may receive real-time status notifications through the website, letting them know if their food is being prepared, is ready for pickup or serving, or if there are any unexpected delays.

Payment and Receipt Process

Once the meal is completed or the order is ready, the payment process is integrated smoothly. MenuMatic generates a digital receipt for the customer through the website. Customers have flexibility in how they pay; they can present this digital receipt to the cashier or directly to the waiter serving their table. The final step involves the restaurant providing the customer with the official physical receipt after the payment is processed, ensuring a complete and convenient transaction.
